Food

mansaf

It is prepared from lamb cooked in yoghurt, and served over a layer of rice and pepper. Add milk sauce to the mansaf and garnish with nuts such as pine nuts and roasted almonds/p>

Maqluba

This dish consists of rice cooked with meat and vegetables such as eggplant, potatoes, and cauliflower.

Musakhan

It features taboon bread topped with chicken cooked with onions, pine nuts, olive oil, and lots of sumac

Al-Makmoura

Prepare boiled meat or chicken, where the meat is cooked with onions and spices until the flavors blend. It is buried under a thick cover of dough, cooked in the oven until well cooked and cut into a triangle shape

Shakriya

Yogurt is used in its preparation. The meat is cooked in a yogurt sauce that contains garlic and spices such as mint and cumin. Shakriya is usually served with white rice or bread

Stuffed zucchini

You can serve stuffed zucchini with tomato sauce or yogurt for a refreshing and delicious taste that balances the flavor of the filling

Stuffed eggplant

It consists of eggplant, chopped itch, tomato paste, chopped onions and some spices

Lahm Bi Ajeen

Minced meat slices are used and mixed with oriental spices and seasonings, then wrapped in a special dough and baked in the oven until browned

Hot fish

Hake is commonly used in its preparation. The spicy fish is characterized by its rich and distinctive taste, as the fish is cooked with tomato sauce, onions and tahini, which gives it a spicy and distinctive flavour

Kofta with tahini

Crepes are one of the most famous French dishes that are popular worldwide. Crepes are characterized by their delicacy and delicious taste, and can be eaten sweet with chocolate, jam, or sugar, or salty with cheese and meat. Crepes are the perfect breakfast or dinner snack and are an essential part of traditional French cuisine.
