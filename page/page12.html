Chilli crab

Crab cooked in a half-thick broth with tomato sauce and chili pepper.

Laksa

Rice noodles in spicy coconut curry soup with shrimp, fish cakes, eggs and chicken meat,

Char kway teow

Fried wide white noodles with black soy sauce, bean sprouts, fish cake, oysters and Chinese sausage

Barbecued stingray

The dish features stinging ray meat topped with a thick sambal sauce, a spicy condiment with diced tomatoes, chili peppers and shrimp paste as main ingredients, then wrapped in banana leaves to be slowly cooked on the grill

Fish head curry

It is a huge fish head and vegetables cooked in curry and served with rice or bread. Its origins go back to southern India, with Chinese and Malaysian influences. In some species, tamarind juice is added to give a sweet, sour taste

Satay

Grilled meat on skewers served with rice cake, peanut sauce, cucumber sauce and chili pepper. It has a strong turmeric aroma and flavor, as this spice is the main seasoning ingredient. The meat is usually either chicken, beef, or lamb

Hawkin Me Shrimp

Fried Hawken noodles with shrimp, chicken or pork chops, squid and fish cake, seasoned with soy sauce, vinegar and chili pepper. Each meal comes with sambal sauce and lemon wedge
